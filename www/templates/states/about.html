Chigago

Chigago (African dodgeball) 1.0.0 is an open source video game for persons of all ages. The video game was inspired by the popular South African recreational game of the same name (aka "Bhathi", "Driepoties", "Chingwii", "Amatin"). It is licensed under the GNU General Public License, version 3 or later. For more, contact chigago.devops@gmail.com.
